# Changelog

## v1.0.0 — Operations Foundation

### Added
- Building and operational task management.
- One accountable owner and required due date per task.
- Priority, status, progress, category, notes, and blocked-reason tracking.
- Filters for personal, overdue, due-this-week, critical, completed, owner, and category views.
- Inline task editing, completion, reopening, and deletion.
- Dashboard Building Accountability tracker with urgent-task preview.
- Luxury quote management and proposal workflow retained.
- Versioned Supabase migration for the Operations Center.

### Preserved
- Calendar and event operations.
- Clients and event-day assignment previews.
- Quotes, payments, floor plans, venue reference, and settings.
